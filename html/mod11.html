The Dark: Code changes

The following are some of the modifications made to Hellfire to produce The Dark. All information here should be applied with the greatest caution, and regarded as examples as how to do things, not as working fixes. More will be added in the future.

Warp your way thru the levels

This change to the Warp spell will let you teleport to the stairs furthest away instead of the one closest by.

change  
:004316E6 C745F4FFFFFF7F mov [ebp-0C], 7FFFFFFF
to  
:004316E6 C745F400000000 mov [ebp-0C], 00000000
and change  
:0043179F 7D09 jge 004317AA
to  
:0043179F 7E09 jle 004317AA

Barbarian with higher endurance

Barbarian gets stunned when damage > clvl*3/2, not clvl*5/4.
change  
:00459F1D 6A04 push 00000004
to  
:00459F1D 6A02 push 00000002

Bard with increased Mana

Bard gets more mana from his items: 3 + 1.5*Magcharacter + 1.75*Magitems + 2*clvl -> ... + 2.5*Magitems + ...
:00424155 C1FF02 sar edi, 02
change to
:00424155 909090 3*nop
