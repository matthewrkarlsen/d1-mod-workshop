Base Item Data

Base item data begins at offset 8E448 (8D408) in Diablo. In Hellfire, it's A05C8.

Each item is 76 bytes in length. Place your cursor at the beginning of an item string and drag it along until you see this at the very bottom "Sel 0x4c". This is one item.
